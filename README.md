This project is based on the concept of digital literacy, which is essential in today’s technology-driven world. As a student digital ambassador, the aim of this project is to understand and demonstrate the importance of using digital tools, online platforms, and communication methods effectively and responsibly.

Task 1- In this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. In my infographic, I included the definition of digital literacy, useful digital tools such as Google Docs, Canva, and GitHub, safe internet practices, and email etiquette
Task 2- In this task, I developed my digital portfolio by creating profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. The purpose of this task was to build a professional online presence and understand how these platforms are used in academics and career development.
Task 3- In this task, I explored coding and collaboration platforms to understand their role in learning and academic work. For the collaboration part, I created a Google Form titled “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz” consisting of five questions. The form included both multiple-choice and short-answer questions to test basic knowledge of digital literacy.
Task 4- In this task, I learned about professional communication and email etiquette. I wrote two formal emails. I also created a social media Do’s and Don’ts checklist to highlight responsible online behavior.
Task 5- In this task, I learned about cybercrime awareness by studying a case of UPI fraud. I understood how fraudsters trick users through fake calls, messages, and payment requests to gain access to their money or personal information.
