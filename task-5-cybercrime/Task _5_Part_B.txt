Part B 
Prevention Checklist: 22. Create a 'Stay Safe Online' checklist
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown or suspicious payment requests  
- Always verify the sender before making any online transaction  
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Regularly check your bank statements for unusual activity  
- Do not save sensitive information on public or shared devices  
- Be cautious of calls or messages creating urgency or fear  
- Immediately report any cyber fraud on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
